Central Dogmas

Dogmas are truths of faith officially proclaimed by the Church, explaining essential aspects of the Catholic faith. They cover not only teachings about Jesus Christ and Mary but also other fundamental truths about God, the sacraments, salvation, the saints, and the Church itself.
